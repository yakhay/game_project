Instructions

In this game, you will participate in five rounds , and each round consists of 10 trials .

During each trial, you will encounter three coins: blue, orange, or yellow.

You have to click on a coin of your choice upon each trial.

Each coin represents a chance to win an amount of money in dirhams.

Round Structure:

Trials: Each round consists of 10 trials.

Coins: You will have to choose between three coins: blue, orange, or yellow.

Winning: Each coin has a potential win associated with it (either 2dh, 5dh or 10dh).

Tax Payment:

At the end of each round, you are required to pay a 20% tax on your earnings.

However, there's an option to skip paying the tax and move to the next round directly.

Auditing Process:

It's important to note that at the end of each round, one out of every 8 participants will be randomly selected for an audit.

If you choose not to pay the tax and get audited, you will proceed to the next round with 0 dirhams in your account, and your earnings will start from scratch.

Consequences of Not Paying Tax:

If you're audited and didn't pay the tax, you'll proceed to the next round with 0 dirhams .

If you're not audited, your decision regarding tax payment will not be checked.

You'll simply continue to the next round with the total sum of money you earned in the round.

Money Accumulation:

The money you accumulate during the five rounds will be credited to your NAPS card.

For instance, if your score at the end of the game is 100, 100 dirhams will be sent to your NAPS account .
